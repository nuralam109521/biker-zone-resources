Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should
know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic
signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your
bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more
focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

The one steadfast rule when it comes to helmets: regardless of wear,
if you crash while wearing a helmet, replace it.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Probably not totally safe. It likely needs air and for various
fittings, nuts and bolts to be checked.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

The seat should be level front to back. The seat height
should be adjusted to allow a slight bend at
the knee when the leg is fully extended.
